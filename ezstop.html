A platform that connects people with different abilities and needs. Its goal is to help users' problems be solved as quickly as possible. It creates a network where everyone can help, share, and ask for help.

introduction

Date: October. 2020
Time: 2 Months
Role: Product Design

EZStop It's a website that allows people to get help more quickly and easily. Users can post their needs to this website, and others who are close to them will see these requirements. Users can contact each other for every requirement on this website, discuss the details, and confirm the order. The goal of EZStop is to give everyone the opportunity to help with earning some money and receive help easily and faster.


The idea for this project came from a casual conversation I had with a friend. I completed the basic research, ideation and simple visual designs by myself.

I also did some expansion from the idea and the design. In the process of learning HTML5 and CSS, I made the basic frame of this website. (Clike Here to See)

Problem

  The type of items which can be delivered is limited, and      the cost for users in remote areas is too high.


When people need something, but it is not convenient for them to go out and buy it. Especially for those people who live in remote places without convenient transportation, they have to wait too long for online shopping; the delivery apps may cost a high service fee, or even no one is willing to take the order because of the location.

For the people who provide delivery services, they may just want to do this for extra money. Being a full-time courier will also face various constraints, including the unfair treatment from the platform.

The existing platforms, such as UberEats, Fantuan Delivery, Doordash and others can only provide specific types of items.

Design Requirements

By considering the users' needs, I identified the key features of the website:

1. A way for users to browse and select existing requirements based on the location.
2. A way for users to post their needs to get help from people nearby.
3. A way for users to contact others.
4. A way for users to get fast and nice customer service. The rights and interests of users can be guaranteed.

Reflection

This project allowed me to learn about UX research, UI design and front-end development. I took advantage of my spare time when I was doing my graduation project to work on the design of this website, so there are some areas I don't have the opportunity to explore.

If I had the opportunity to continue this project, I would first conduct user testings in order to observe users' attitudes, problems in the process of using and users' opinions about the design concept. My approach was biased. I need to know whether users accept this design of delivery service and then make more improvements according to the test results.
