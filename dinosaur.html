introduction

Date: Dec. 2018
Time: 2 Months
Role: Product Design

Dinosaur & Museum is a mobile game designed for the Dinosaur Exhibition at the Inner Mongolia Museum in China. This game can help to improve the visiting experience. It brings users an easier and more interesting way to visit the exhibition, learn knowledge, even socialize. This game is also a promotional tool for the exhibition and the museum.

In this project, I was responsible for most of the work, including the research, analysis, ideation, and part of the visuals of the game.

Research

I began the project by interviewing visitors aged between 6 - 35 years old in the exhibition to understand their needs and preferences.

Pain Points

Through my research, I realized that my interviewees:

1. Find it difficult to understand the explanatory text on labels next to the exhibits.

2. Get tired and bored since the traditional visiting method - reading, walking, watching.

3. Can't remember the knowledge and information even though they just read the labels.

Problem &
Design Requirements

I summarized a problem statement by using the research and synthesis as the guideline:

 The learning process for an exhibition needs to be more  interesting and efficient, also should be more continuous  and interactive.


Based on the problem, I developed a list of design requirements for solving the problem.
This mobile game is supposed to:

1. Help users to understand the information.
2. Encourage users to explore and interact.
3. Attract users for multiple visits.
4. Help users to memorize, review and apply the knowledge.

Ideation

Storyboards

A mobile game that the dinosaur exhibition visitors can check the knowledge about all exhibits. They can play the game with others to enhance their learning. This product can be used as a teaching tool in the museum and at home.

Wireframes

Reflection

In this project, I realized that the design should focus more on the problem that needs to be solved. I connected the game with the museum exhibition through the game, finding and unlocking dinosaurs, and dinosaur info page throughout this project. If I were to continue this project, I would add more museum-oriented functions to promote the museum.

In designing and testing, I realized the text information was still a little boring. Adding some animation or colourful pictures would attract users, especially young users, and let them take longer to read and learn.
